Macaroni Corn

  • 1 can cream corn
  • 1 can whole kernel corn
  • 1 cup Velveta cheese
  • 1 cup macaroni
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Salt and pepper
Grilled Cheese

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl, then pour out into a greased 9x13 inch pan.
